Our Mission

The UMass Robotics Club exists to create an inclusive, hands-on environment where students of all majors and experience levels can explore robotics. We aim to bridge theory and practice through collaborative engineering, research partnerships, and competitive builds that challenge members to grow technically and professionally.

Our Teams

Our club is structured into focused teams that allow members to dive deep into specific areas of robotics.

Research Team

Collaborates with the Dynamic & Autonomous Robotics Systems Lab to develop advanced quadruped robotics for research applications. Members work on controls, hardware integration, and autonomy.

Ant Weight BattleBots

A beginner-friendly competition team where members design, build, and battle small robots. Emphasis is placed on learning mechanical design, electronics, and teamwork.
