UMass Robotics Club

Who We Are

The UMass Robotics Club is a welcoming space for all students—no matter their major or experience level—to explore the world of robotics.

Our projects are hands-on and cross-disciplinary, spanning mechanical design, software development, electronics, and creative problem-solving.

What You’ll Learn

  • Mechanical design & CAD
  • Embedded systems & electronics
  • Software, controls, and autonomy
  • Teamwork, leadership, and engineering thinking
